About Season

The intelligent kitchen companion.

Most recipe apps ignore the calendar. Season is built around it. Every recipe, every ingredient, every suggestion is filtered through a single question: is this what the earth is offering right now?

We've built a curated ingredient catalog that understands seasonality, locality, and taste — and grows smarter over time.

How it works

Simple by design.

1
See what's in season
Open Season and discover the ingredients at their freshest right now, wherever you are in the year.
2
Find or import a recipe

3
Cook, save, repeat
Save what you loved. Build your fridge. Let Season remember your pantry so next week starts one step ahead.
Smart Import

From social caption
to your kitchen.

Saw a recipe on Instagram but don't want to type it all out? Just paste the caption. Season's AI reads it, extracts the ingredients, steps, and timing, and creates a clean recipe card — ready to cook.

Works with messy captions, mixed languages, and hashtag-heavy posts. If the recipe is in there, Season will find it.
