Method

01

Roast the Squash

Toss diced butternut squash with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast at 200°C for 25 minutes until caramelized and tender. Set aside half for blending into the stock for extra creaminess.

02

Char the Sage

In a small pan, heat a knob of butter. Fry the fresh sage leaves until crispy and slightly darkened. Remove and drain on paper towels. This oil will carry the intense sage aroma into the risotto base.

03

The Risotto Base

Sauté finely chopped shallots in the sage butter. Add Arborio rice and toast for 2 minutes. Gradually add hot stock, one ladle at a time, stirring constantly until the rice is al dente and creamy.

04

Final Finish

Fold in the roasted squash cubes, blended squash purée, and plenty of Parmesan. Season generously. Garnish with the charred sage leaves and a final crack of black pepper.
